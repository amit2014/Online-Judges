Getting Started

At Facebook's core is the social graph; people and the connections they have to each other and everything they care about. The Facebook Platform is the set of APIs and tools which enable you to enhance your website, application, or device with the social graph.

Integrating with the Facebook Platform will help you drive growth and deeper engagement by personalizing the experience for your users and letting them share activity with the people who matter in their lives:

Growth: Integrating with the Facebook Platform helps your application grow by making it easy for your users to share with their friends. Our Distribution Channels, such as the Like Button, to enable your users to share activity in your application or invite their friends to your application, often with a single click.

Engagement: Everything is better with your friends. Facebook Platform makes it easy for users to bring their friends and their interests to your application. You can start with simple Social Plugins, such as the Recommendation or Activity plugins, to highlight relevant social activity on your site. You can also integrate with the Graph API to deeply personalize your application based on the likes, interests, and friends of the current user.

If you’re new to the Facebook Platform, you should start with the Getting Started guides organized around the major types of applications you can build on the Facebook Platform, including Websites, Mobile Apps, and Apps on Facebook.com. If you’re looking for inspiration, take a look at some of the sites and applications that have integrated with Facebook in our Showcase.

Key Concepts

Graph API
The Graph API is the core of Facebook Platform, enabling you to read and write data to Facebook. It provides a simple and consistent view of the social graph, uniformly representing objects (like people, photos, events, and pages) and the connections between them (like friendships, likes, and photo tags).
Authentication
Facebook authentication enables your application to interact with the Graph API on behalf of Facebook users, and it provides a powerful single-sign on mechanism across Web, mobile, and desktop apps.
Social plugins
Social plugins enable you to provide engaging social experiences to your users with just a line of HTML. Because the plugins are served by Facebook, the content is personalized to the viewer whether or not they have signed into your site.
Open Graph protocol
The Open Graph protocol enables you to integrate your pages into the social graph. These pages gain the functionality of other graph objects including profile links and stream updates for connected users.

Tools

Live Status
Having unexpected problems with our APIs? Things suddenly stopped working? It may be due to our platform itself. Before contacting us, check here to see if we know about it, and if its affecting everybody.
Insights
Insights provides analytics on your Facebook Page, application and website. The Insights Dashboard makes it easy to see how Facebook users are interacting with your content, and the Insights APIs allow developers to obtain additional Insights and integrate the data with third party reporting systems.
Test Users
Our test user feature allows you to create user accounts to test your applications. You can create one account to use for ad-hoc testing or many accounts for automated testing.
JavaScript Test Console
Run and debug methods from our Javascript SDK right in your browser. It also includes a large number of examples.
URL Linter
The URL Linter allows you to see how Facebook views and parses your web pages. This is useful to validate any Open Graph protocol tags embedded in your pages. Using the URL Linter also allows you to force Facebook to refresh its cache of your page.
